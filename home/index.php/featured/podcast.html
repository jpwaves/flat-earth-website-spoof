Flat Earth Podcast

This is the Flat Earth Society Podcast, a place or free thinkers to discuss alternative science and new or unusual ways of viewing the world. It's hosted by Daniel Shenton and Michael Wilmore. We've been on hiatus for a while (sorry about that) but we're looking to continue producing new podcasts in the very near future. If you have suggestions for guests you'd like to hear on the podcast, please contact us and we'll try our best to make it happen!

The Flat Earth Society Podcast is available on iTunes as well as on Podbean and our website (below).

The Flat Earth Society Podcast - Episode 4 with guest Hollow Earth expert Professor Darryl Jones

Dec 12th, 2012

Our long, long-awaited fourth episode! Sorry about the delay on this. The next one will be along much faster!

In this episode, Michael N. Wilmore sits down with Professor Darryl Jones for a in-depth discussion of Hollow Earth literature and its connection to Flat Earth theory. An excellent companion-piece to our previous episode with Hollow Earth theorist Rodney Cluff.

The Flat Earth Society Podcast - Episode 3 with guest Hollow Earth theorist Rodney Cluff

Sep 16th, 2012

Finally, the long-awaited third episode of the Flat Earth Society podcast! Daniel and Michael chat with Rodney Cluff from OurHollowEarth, arguably the world's foremost Hollow Earth theorist. We talk with Rodney about Hollow Earth theory, religion, his early years.. and, of course, Star Trek.

Please keep an eye out for episode 4 in the next week or two -- we promise!

The Flat Earth Society Podcast - Episode 2 with guest Duncan Lunan (part 2)

Jul 31st, 2012

Our second episode is the conclusion of our interview with author and astronomer Duncan Lunan. In this final segment, we discuss science fiction and some of the freedoms provided by living on the fringes of mainstream science.

Keep an eye out for episode 3 in the very near future. Daniel and Michael will be chatting with Rodney Cluff, a believer in Hollow Earth theory!

The Flat Earth Society Podcast - Episode 1 with guest Duncan Lunan

Jul 11th, 2012

This is the first episode of the Flat Earth Society Podcast. Flat Earth Society President Daniel Shenton and Vice President Michael N. Wilmore interview author and astronomer Duncan Lunan about topics including the Black Knight satellite, life on the fringes of mainstream science and his new book, Children From The Sky, an account of two mysterious children appearing on the outskirts of a medieval British village.
