Welcome to the Flat Earth Society Library

This is the largest collection of Flat Earth literature on the internet. I believe we also have the largest collection of Flat Earth Society newsletters anywhere in the world, online or in print.

Our collection is divided into the following categories.

Books

Complete books or excerpts from books such as Zetetic Astronomy and Adrian Galileo.

Pamphlets & Journals

An extensive collection of short works, usually in the form of pamphlets, journals, or tracts. We have many issues of Earth Not A Globe Review and The Earth.

Letters & Notes

Notes and incoming/outgoing correspondence from the Samuel Shenton Archives.

Newsletters

Our collection of Flat Earth Society newsletters from the 1970's - 1990's

Newspapers & Magazines

Articles about the Flat Earth Society from newspapers and magazines.

Press Releases & Promotional

Our press releases and promotional materials.

Web Articles

Articles about the Flat Earth Society from websites and online sources.

SOME FEATURED AUTHORS

Samuel Shenton
Former President , Flat Earth Society
Samuel Shenton (March 1903 – 2 March 1971) was the founder in 1956 of the Flat Earth Society, based in Dover, England. Having been trusted with Samuel Shenton's personal collection of works, letters, lecture posters, and materials we have the largest collection of Shenton based literature anywhere.

Charles K. Johnson
Former President, Flat Earth Society
Weighing in from the religious side of things, Charles K Johnson believed in an infinite plane based on accounts from Moses. Our collection features several interviews and articles by this former President.
Robert Schadewald
Member
Member of the former Flat Earth Society and author of the book "Worlds Of Their Own", Schadewald was an expert in Flat Earth theory and wrote quite a few articles, some featured here, on it.
